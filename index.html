Synopsys Armenia

Synopsys Armenia provides R&D and product support for electronic design automation (EDA), design for manufacturing (DFM), and semiconductor intellectual property (IP) solutions. It is one of the largest IT employers in Armenia with more than 1000 employees. With two locations in Yerevan, and one in Gyumri, Synopsys Armenia is also one of the largest Synopsys sites outside the U.S.
